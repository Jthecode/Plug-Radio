Execution Flow

How to build a stronger radio programming system

This is a clean framework for turning your shows into a more professional station operation.

Step 01
Lock the flagship shows

Choose the top three to four show formats that define the station identity and make them recurring.

Step 02
Assign hosts and times

Give every block a clear host, time, and purpose so the station feels organized and dependable.

Step 03
Promote each show individually

Break every show into its own promo cycle using Instagram reels, flyers, clips, and booking announcements.

Step 04
Turn interviews into station assets

Repurpose live conversations into social content, highlight reels, and featured pages that keep value compounding.
