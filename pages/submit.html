Submission Standards

What artists should include before sending music

The cleaner the submission, the easier it is for Plug Radio to understand the release, connect it to the right opportunity, and decide whether it fits a feature, interview, station push, or future spotlight.

01

Send your strongest record

Make sure the title is correct, the primary link works, and the record reflects how you want to be represented.

02

Include context around the release

Explain whether this is a single, rollout, interview pitch, feature request, or part of a bigger project push.

03

Add socials and contact details

Give Plug Radio enough information to follow up, tag your artist page correctly, and understand the bigger picture around the record.

Best Practices

What helps a submission stand out

  • Clear artist name and final track title
  • One strong main music link
  • Professional release context or rollout note
  • Instagram handle and active contact email
  • A simple explanation of what you want from Plug Radio

For Artists

How to improve your chances

Send complete and professional information the first time. The cleaner the package, the easier it is for a station team to take the record seriously and respond with less friction.

Do Not Forget

Quick checklist before pressing submit

  • Double-check every music and press link
  • Make sure the record title is final and correct
  • Use a real contact email you actively monitor
  • Include an Instagram handle for tagging and follow-up
